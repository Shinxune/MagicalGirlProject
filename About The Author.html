Hey!

I’m Shinxune your happy-go-lucky Kitsune,

I’ve been streaming video games and making things on the internet since 2017, but I’ve been writing and drawing for most of my life. I love creating characters and worlds and bringing them to life with my writing and drawing!

The Magical Girl Project is a idea that came from working on my RP channel, one of my characters was going to be a magical girl, but then it became its own story, and now here we are! This is one of the many stories I hope to share with you all!
